Privacy Policy for yNotes

Data Collection and Use yNotes is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, notes, or biometric data to external servers.

Local Storage All your notes, books, and encrypted data in the "Safe Zone" are stored locally on your device. Biometric authentication (like fingerprint) is handled directly by your device's operating system, and the app never accesses your actual fingerprint data.

Changes to This Policy If we update our Privacy Policy, we will post the changes on this page.

Contact Us If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
